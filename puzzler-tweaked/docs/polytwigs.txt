  <../solutions/twigs/pentatwigs-mobius-strip-1.txt>`__

  .. image:: images/twigs/pentatwigs-mobius-strip-1.png

  Take the puzzle strip above, give one end a half-twist, and join it
  to the other end, resulting in a `Möbius strip`__, a surface with
  only one side and one edge.

  __ http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/M%C3%B6bius_strip

  This puzzle was implemented without any coordinate "wrap-around": a
  piece cannot begin at the right edge and continue (through the join)
  to the left edge.  There may be many more solutions with such a
  wrap-around configuration.

* _`Four congruent groups`:

  Colin F. Brown posed this problem: divide the twelve pentatwigs into
  four groups of three, add one of the tetratwigs to each group.  Now
  find a 19-line region that each (equally divided) group will tile.

  Mr. Brown provided one solution:

  .. figure:: images/twigs/polytwigs-45-four-congruent-1.png

     Example 1 of four congruent groups from the tetratwigs &
     pentatwigs: `19 solutions
     <../solutions/twigs/polytwigs-45-four-congruent-1.txt>`__.

  Others are possible:

  .. figure:: images/twigs/polytwigs-45-four-congruent-2.png

     Example 2 of four congruent groups from the tetratwigs &
     pentatwigs: `38 solutions
     <../solutions/twigs/polytwigs-45-four-congruent-2.txt>`__.

  **Open Problem: Combined Symmetrical Shape**

  Find a solution to the four congruent groups problem such that the
  four groups can be combined into a symmetrical shape.  The more
  symmetries the better.

  The best case I have been able to come up with so far:

  .. figure:: images/twigs/polytwigs-45-four-congruent-3.png

     Example 3 of four congruent groups from the tetratwigs &
     pentatwigs: `2 solutions
     <../solutions/twigs/polytwigs-45-four-congruent-3.txt>`__.

  .. figure:: images/twigs/polytwigs-45-four-congruent-3-combined.png

     Example 3, combined: one symmetry (180° rotational); `24 solutions
     when restricted to combinations of the congruent shapes above
     <../solutions/twigs/polytwigs-45-four-congruent-3-combined.txt>`__; many
     solutions if unrestricted.
